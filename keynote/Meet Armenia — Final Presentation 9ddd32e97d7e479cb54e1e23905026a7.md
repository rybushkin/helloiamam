## 1. The Opportunity

**Armenia is entering a new visibility window.**

Tourism is growing. Direct access from London is launching. Smaller, less obvious destinations are gaining global attention. Armenia is moving closer to Europe through the EU visa liberalisation process.

The timing is right for Armenia to become more than a place people discover by accident.

It can become a country people feel connected to before they travel.

## 10. The Business Model

**Content → Emotion → Intent → Trip**

Meet Armenia is designed as a media-first travel company.

**1. Content**  

Short videos, carousels, guides, stories, maps, newsletters, and cultural formats.

**2. Emotion**  

The audience begins to feel the country, not just learn facts about it.

**3. Intent**  

People start saying: “I want to go there”, “I want to try this”, “I want this route”.

**4. Trip**  

The brand converts attention into routes, partners, bookings, tours, and local products.

Main principle:

Media brand first.  

Travel company second.

Revenue channels:

- affiliate and commission from travel bookings
- curated trips and city breaks
- wine, food, culture, and nature routes
- paid guides and premium itineraries
- brand partnerships
- sponsored cultural content
- merchandise and printed guides
- B2B white-label products for tourism boards, airlines, hotels, and cultural partners

## 11. Go-To-Market

**Start with attention. Convert later.**

**Phase 1: Media Proof**  

Launch core rubrics: Wine, Duduk, Lavash, Mountains, Hidden Armenia, Yerevan.

**Phase 2: Audience & Data**  

Measure what people save, share, comment on, and click.

**Phase 3: Trip Intent**  

Launch mini-guides, email, Telegram, and web-based route recommendations.

**Phase 4: Travel Products**  

Build partner routes, curated tours, city breaks, and cultural packages.

**Phase 5: Platform / IP**  

Turn the model into a repeatable system for other countries and regions.

The sequence is simple:

Attention first.  

Data second.  

Travel products third.
